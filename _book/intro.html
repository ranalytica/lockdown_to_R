Lockdown to R-Full-stack

1 Beginning

It was March 2020, I was still traumatize that Andrew Yang drop-out for the presidential race. The other headline news during these time frame was about Syrian Civil War, Coronavirus pandemic, European migrant crisis, and the presidential race in the United States (Wikipedia 2020).

I was very optimistic that United States will not have a pandemic in any of its states or properties. At the same time, I was amazed at the many dashboards that were created to monitor the Covid-19. Curiosity got the best of me and I decided to sign-up for the Coursera - Johns Hopkins University Data Science Specialization (Johns Hopkins University 2013).
